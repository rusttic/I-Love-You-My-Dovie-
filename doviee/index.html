Why I Like You

🌸 🌸 🌸 🌸

I like you because of your kindness — the effortless, instinctive kind that doesn't ask for anything in return. The way you notice when someone needs a little extra warmth and you just quietly give it. That's not common. That's something rare.

I like you because of your smile. Not just how it looks — though it genuinely could stop time — but the way it makes the people around you feel. Safe. Seen. Lighter than they were a moment before.

I like you because of your personality. The wit that catches me off guard, the depth that keeps surprising me, the way you can be silly one minute and thoughtful the next. You're never one thing. You're a whole world.

I like you because of how caring you are. You remember the small things. You check in. You hold space for people without being asked. You love loudly with your actions and quietly with your presence.

I like you because of what you make possible. Not just in your own life — in the lives of everyone around you. You have this quiet way of making people feel like they can do more, be more.

And I like you simply because being around you makes everything a little more worth it. The mornings feel lighter. The jokes feel funnier. The ordinary days feel like something to actually hold onto. That's a gift. That's you.
